Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about the web design and development process

How long does it take to build a website? +

It typically takes **3-5 days** to build and launch a standard website once we have gathered all content and branding details. If you require premium or unlimited pages, the timeline will depend on project scope, but basic pages can be fast-tracked in 2-3 days.

Do you offer maintenance? +

Yes, absolutely! I offer monthly maintenance plans ranging from **₹2,000 to ₹5,000/month** (or $100 to $250/month) to handle regular content changes, update images, perform technical bug fixes, monitor security, and conduct weekly performance audits.

Do you help with domain and hosting? +

Yes, I guide you step-by-step through the process. I will help you select, purchase, and set up your domain name and web hosting providers (such as Hostinger, Namecheap, Vercel, or Netlify) to ensure they are configured correctly for high speeds and low costs.

Can you redesign my existing website? +

Yes. I can redesign your existing website to give it a modern look, improve loading speeds, resolve structural issues, and ensure it is fully mobile responsive. This is a great way to boost your visitor conversion rate.

Do you work with international clients? +

Yes, I work with clients globally. Payments from outside India can be made securely via **Stripe, PayPal, or direct bank transfer**. All communication can be carried out smoothly via WhatsApp, email, or video calls.
